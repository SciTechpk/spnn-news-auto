Under section 7, the marriage under the alashing custom, (special custom of marriage) shall be considered valid in case where a Kalash girl marries a Kalash man of her own will, without prior consent but before consummation of marriage, the girl’s parents or close relatives visit her in-laws house, to verify and confirm her consent and happiness for the marriage.
