The government then applies a 10 per cent customs duty and adds the margins for oil marketing companies and dealers, along with the petroleum levy. This combined formula determines the retail price of petroleum products for consumers. Miftah Ismail wrote that the government’s reasoning is understandable. However, he noted that domestic prices for petroleum products for March 1–15 had already been revised just a week earlier, on February 28, using the average import price for the second half of February.
